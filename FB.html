JOIN OUR FACEBOOK GROUP!


We have recently launched a private Facebook Group for resident Garden Charge-payers!

Whilst this website will remain our main platform, we encourage you to join as we will publish periodic garden updates there too, plus occasional news of garden events. We will not be curating a huge amount of content but hope for some of you it will be an easier platform to keep updated on. Nor will we have time to moderate your chats so will require you to abide by the fairly standard groups rules and online etiquette, but hope you may meet like minded neighbours and we hope it will encourage a greater sense of community!

Membership is by invite only, so if you are a Garden Charge-payer and would like to join, please contact us to request an invite.

Please note that membership will be stopped should you no longer remain a Garden Charge-payer.
