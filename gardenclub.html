GARDEN CLUB!



New Sunday morning fun for garden key holders with
the parent & child Garden Club!

Prise your children off the iPads and sign them up for some wholesome nature-loving fun - the new exciting Sunday Garden Club in Earl's Court Square garden for key-holders with their children.

Come and join in for an hour of craft, gardening and muddy activities. Garden Club will be planting, drawing, going on insect safaris plus constructing mini-wormeries and designing tiny Earl’s Court Square cress gardens.

No Sunday Best, there will be mud, paint - and Haribo prizes for enthusiastic students.

Garden Club will provide all materials and activities for free, but you (the parent) need to participate fully in all the activities (drawing, collage, planting, treasure hunts) and look after and chaperone your child/children for the entire class. This is not a coffee morning for the grown ups while the children get to work - you’ll need to get involved and supervise your child.

Looking forward to seeing you there!

Who can attend

The children of Earl’s Court Square garden key holders aged 3-10, plus a fully participating parent (key holder) acting as each child’s PA.
One parent per child, or one parent per child and its siblings.

How it works

Parents should sign up below for each individual session, first come first served.
A maximum of 10-15 children per session (plus their parents).

Cost

Totally free!

Dates and Times

10am - 11am/11.30am on select Sundays, usually every fortnight, please check session dates below.
No club during August, Christmas or January, and if it rains Garden Club will cancel on the day.

Health & Safety

Sessions may be cancelled due to Covid-19
All garden activities are subject to the Government's latest Covid-19 guidelines.
You will be asked to complete a Covid Health declaration during each sessions sign up.
Participation in activities and use of the garden is completely at your own risk.

Rules

Parents are responsible for the behaviour of their own children at all times and must adhere to the published garden byelaws.

Wait List

Garden Club aims to close a sessions online registration when a session becomes fully subscribed. Please be understanding if you are moved to the wait list as the club is for free and managed in the organisers spare time.

Cancellation

If you can’t make it, please cancel your child’s place at least 24 hours before the club so others can take the slot.

How to cancel:
Kindly follow the link on the relevant session's confirmation email you received to return to your existing registration. Uncheck the session date you applied for and resubmit the form.
