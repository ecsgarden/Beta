GARDEN CLUB!

New Sunday fun for garden key holders with the parent and child Garden Club!

Prise your children off the iPads and sign them up for some wholesome nature-loving fun - our new exciting Sunday Garden Club in Earl's Court Garden Square for key-holders and their children. Come and join in for an hour of craft, gardening and muddy activities. We will be planting, drawing, going on insect safaris plus constructing mini-wormeries and designing tiny Earl’s Court Square cress gardens.

No Sunday Best, there will be mud, paint - and Haribo prizes for enthusiastic students.

We will provide all materials and activities for free, but we need the you (the parent) to participate fully in all the activities (drawing, collage, planting, treasure hunts) and look after and chaperone your child/children for the entire class. This is not a coffee morning for the grown ups while the children get to work - you’ll need to get involved and supervise your child.

Looking forward to seeing you there!



Who can attend: The children of Earl’s Court Square garden key holders aged 3-10, plus a fully participating parent (key holder) acting as each child’s PA.
One parent per child, or one parent per child and its siblings.

How it works: Parents should sign up below for each individual session, first come first served.
There will be 10-15 children per session (plus their parents).

Cost: totally free!

Dates and Times: 10am - 11am/11.30am on select Sundays, usually every fortnight, please check session dates below.
No club during August, Christmas or January, and if it rains we’ll cancel on the day.

Wait List: We aim to close registrations when a session becomes fully subscribed, but please be understanding if we contact you saying you have been moved to a wait list as we run this for free in our spare time so might be late closing a session's registration.

Cancellation: If you can’t make it, please cancel your child’s place at least 24 hours before the club so others can take the slot.
How to cancel: Kindly follow the link on the relevant session's confirmation email you received to return to your existing registration. Uncheck the session date you applied for and resubmit the form.
